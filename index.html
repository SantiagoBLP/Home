About Me

My research revolves around synthesizing and characterizing groundbreaking graphene-derived nanomaterials. These materials exhibit vast potential across a spectrum of applications, ranging from water contaminant degradation to fluorescence-based ion detection, efficient photoanodes, and electrochemical glucose sensing.

My Research and Projects

I have worked on several key projects, including the development of Twisted Bilayer Graphene and participation in Horizon 2020 EU Projects such as MELON & ULTIMATE-I. My work focuses on producing and synthesizing novel graphene-derived nanomaterials for various electronic applications.
